Accidental Death & Dismemberment

Financial Protection after a serious incident

Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D) plans are a form of life insurance that offers coverage for your entire life. AD&D plans pay a benefit to your beneficiaries in the event you are killed or dismembered because of a covered accident. Click the button below to learn more about this type of life insurance.

    Key Benefits of AD&D Insurance

  • Pays policy face amount to beneficiary in the event of death
  • Scheduled benefit for dismemberment
  • More affordable than term life with similar limits
  • No medical exam insurance options
  • Can be used to supplement a life insurance policy

Accidental Death & Dismemberment

Financial protection after a serious incident

Where would your family be if you died in a car accident tomorrow? How would you cope if you lost a limb after an accident at work?

Accidental death & dismemberment (AD&D) insurance plans cover unlikely events, such as dying in an accident versus dying from an illness. As a result, these insurance plans are fairly inexpensive. They pay full benefits to your beneficiaries if you die and benefits to you if you are dismembered. AD&D are substantially less expensive than other types of life insurance because they pay benefits only in the result of an accidental death or dismemberment.

    Do I Really Need an AD&D Plan?

    You should consider an AD&D plan if you:

  • Want to be sure your family is protected if you are in an accident
  • Would like to supplement other life insurance or disability coverages
  • Feel underinsured and are on a tight budget
  • Don’t have any life insurance coverage
  • Just want some financial protection in the event of a serious incident
