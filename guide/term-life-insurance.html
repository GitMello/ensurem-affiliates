Term Life Insurance

Affordable Coverage to Protect Your Family’s Future

Term Life Insurance is a form of life insurance that provides affordable protection for a specified period of time (the term). Term life insurance allows you to choose the coverage amount that is right for you, with premium payments that do not change for the term of the policy. Term life insurance is flexible, qualifying term policies can be converted to a permanent life or universal life insurance policy.

    Key Benefits of Term Life Insurance

  • Extremely affordable premiums
  • Instant approval options available
  • Up to $10 million benefit for your family
  • No medical exam insurance options
  • May be converted to permanent policy

    Common uses for Term Life Insurance

  • Help provide for a family's loss
  • Cover short-term debts and needs
  • Provide additional insurance protection during the child-raising years
  • Help cover specific financial reponsibilities like a mortgage or college expenses
  • Supplment a permanent policy or work policy

    Life stages where Term Life Insurance should be considered...

  • Early and/or lean working years
  • Getting married
  • Having your first child

    • During these life stages when budget is a primary concern, term life insurance can provide valuable coverage at an affordable price.
